Why worry about carbon emission?


  • Is the primary greenhouse gas emitted through human activities
  • Traps heat into the atmosphere
  • Alters local climate temperatures which affects forests, crop yields, and water supplies

    • Why worry about carbon emission from transportation?


  • Transportation accounts for 32% of total U.S. emissions
  • Amount of CO2 that one gallon of gas produces: 19.4 pounds
  • The number of vehicle miles traveled by passenger cars has increased by 35% from 1990-2012
  • The average passenger car in the United States produces just under 1 pound of carbon dioxide per mile traveled.
